Moses Lee

full-stack developer | financier | yogi

Moses began his career at Fortress Group, a global fund placement firm representing private equity, venture capital, and growth equity funds. As an Analyst, he was responsible for assessing the viability of potential GP client fundraises, conducting research on the alternative investment criteria and portfolio objectives of institutional investors, and drafting marketing and due diligence materials. There he developed exceptional personal responsibility and problem solving, as he worked with a team of analysts, associates, and managing directors.

While conducting market research for a growth equity fund manager with a focus on artifical intelligence, he became fascinated by machine learning, blockchain, and cryptography. Moses decided he wanted to learn more about the technical knowledge and programming involved. He then enrolled in a full immersive coding program, located in the Atlanta Tech Village, the fourth largest tech hub in America. It has accelerated his learning and building of a strong programming foundation in which he continues to expand upon.

Moses revels being at the cross section of technology and finance because of the large impact and positive influence it may have on society. He earned his B.B.A. in Finance and is a Certified Alternative Investment Analyst (CAIA) Charterholder. He resides in Atlanta and enjoys coding, traveling, and yoga.
